Classic Lasagna

Following these ingredients and recipes you will be able to make one of the most classic italian dishes, Lasgna!

ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly satled water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degreesF (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghettis sauve and garlic and simmer 5 minutes
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. in 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated over for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
